Our Story

Nurturing Hope, One Child at a Time

Founded on the belief that geography shouldn't define a child's potential, CDAK has been at the forefront of development in Kafue for over a decade. We combine professional expertise with deep local roots to address the multifaceted challenges facing our youth.

Our holistic approach ensures that we don't just provide aid, but build sustainable systems of support that empower families and transform entire communities.
