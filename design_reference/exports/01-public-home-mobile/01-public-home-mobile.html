Rooted in Community, Driven by Heart

Child Development Agency Kafue (CDAK) is a grassroots organization dedicated to transforming lives. Since our inception, we have worked hand-in-hand with local leaders, families, and educators to create a protective environment where every child can flourish.

Our approach is holistic—addressing health, education, and safety to ensure that no child in Kafue is left behind. Through your support, we bridge the gap between vulnerability and opportunity.

"Community is at the core of everything we do."

Our Focus Areas

We implement diverse programs designed to provide sustainable solutions for the challenges children face today.

Education

Education Support

Providing scholarships, uniforms, and learning materials to ensure every child has a desk.

85% of Annual Goal Reached

Safety

Child Protection

Establishing safe houses and counseling centers for vulnerable children in the community.

Health

Health & Nutrition

Mobile clinics and nutrition programs providing essential care and food security for families.

Development

Skills Development

Vocational training for youth to ensure they have the tools for economic independence.

Leadership

Youth Empowerment

Mentorship programs fostering leadership and civic engagement among young people.

Infrastructure

Community Development

Improving local infrastructure, including clean water access and community spaces.
